Leadership

The Team Behind 200+ Years of Experience

Aldrin October
Group CEO
MBA (Henley) · M.Sc Coaching & Behavioural Change (Reading)

Aldrin is the Group CEO of Optifect Solutions (Pty) Ltd, a Marketing Graduate and Certified Business Coach with more than 20 years of corporate experience at Nampak, Saint-Gobain, and Afrisam. He served on various EXCOs, held General Management roles, and is Managing Director of Capital Cement Distributors, a Division of the Afrisam Group. He has a passion for Business Management, Sales & Marketing, Human Capital, and Business Development.

Rozanne October
Director — Education & Training
B.Ed (UWC) · M.Ed in Educational Management & Leadership (Wits)

Rozanne is a Director of Optifect Solutions (Pty) Ltd with more than 20 years in Education and Training. An Accredited Assessor and Facilitator, she has led major training projects including the GPLMS Programme, E-Learning for Vastratech, and work with Macmillan Education and multiple NGOs. Her passion is the advancement of individuals through education in marginalised communities.

Patricia Gumede
Associate
B.Com Hons (Fort Hare) · Industrial Relations (Wits Business School)

Patricia brings 35 years of corporate experience, having held Executive and Director-level positions at Primedia as Group Human Resource Executive and at Saint Gobain S.A. as Human Resources Director. She has served on several Boards as a non-executive member and has spent the past five years as an Executive Consultant across industries, specialising in Human Capital and Business Management.
